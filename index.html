About us

Our Story

Apex Horticulture is a wholesome brand centered on developing sustainable communities through landscape design, innovative agricultural practices and general consultancy.

Recognizing the importance of horticulture for its environmental and ornamental benefits, improvement of quality of life and our environment, and for functional uses, such as energy conservation, pollution amelioration, oxygen production and food-security.

Moving forward, urbanization is inevitable and at this revelation our goal is to encourage Nigerians to utilize their immediate environment "with little or no land you can still grow plants" , one potted plant per 100 square feet of indoor space in an average home or office was sufficient to cleanse the air of pollutants, ornamental plants can be strategically located to engineer a more pleasant environment in which to live in also medicinal plants can be incorporated into these sustainable gardens as a means of encouraging citizens to adopt more healthier lifestyles and eventually switch to alternative medicine.

Our company CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) focal point will be constructing gardens for orphanages, teaching them young how to cultivate/ grow plants and nurture life. Also our sustainable application is in view (Agro-Tech) where people can order a variety of plants species for both consumption and ornamental purposes. Furthermore people can book our consultation/landscaping and horticulture services on the mobile application.

Finally we humans, have lost touch and certainty about our relationship with mother nature. Therefore there is need for more emphasis on horticulture as a viable industry in the ever changing landscapes of our man-made world and the natural environment.
